Food & Drink

Snacks & Beverages

Hot Snacks

  • Cheese & Bacon Burger: A 1/4 pounder angus steak burger with Monterey Jack Cheese, Lettuce, Tomato and Bacon with tomato relish. Served on a sesame seed bun
  • Meditteranean Veggie Burger: A patty made from red peppers, olives, sundried tomatoes and mozarella with lettuce and mayo. Served on a sesame seed bun
  • Chilli Cheese Hot Dog: The Classic Cinema snack. A hot dog served with chilli and grated cheddar
  • Nachos: Toasted tortillas with guacamole, sour cream and spicy tomato salsa topped with mozarella & cheddar
  • Pizza: A large slice of either margherita or pepperoni pizza
  • Homemade Fries: Thin cut seasoned fries

Other Snacks

  • Pick and mix stand with over 30 sweets to choose from. Indulge your sweet tooth.
  • Sweet, buttered, toffee or salted popcorn
  • Mini pringle tubs
  • Ben & Jerrys Ice cream

Beverages

Pepsi, Diet Pepsi, Fanta, Irn Bru, Sprite, and still or sparkling water.
